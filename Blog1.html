Introduction To POWER BI

Introduction to PowerBI

Power BI is a business analytics service introduced by Microsoft that delivers insights to enable fast, informed decisions. You can Transform data into stunning visuals and share them with colleagues on any device and visually explore and analyze data—on-premises and in the cloud—all in one view. Additionally you can collaborate on and share customized dashboards and interactive reports. Also, scale across your organization with built-in governance and security.

Clean and mash-up your DATA

It is important to clean up the data before mving forward. This can be achieved by consolidate data from a broad range of sources or merge or append queries to combine data from multiple queries into a single query and transforming data to fit your needs using intuitive UI. You should only select data for inclusion, cleanse data and remove errors and define calculations to generate new fields for use in reports and develop advanced analytics using a combination of measures and relationships. Uncover correlations, highlight exceptions and understand business outcomes

Explore your DATA

Next step is exploring data in a wide variety of ways and across multiple visualizations using drag and drop canvas ,digging deeper into your reports, drilling-down in your hierarchical data, filtering, sorting, hover over and highlight data, leveraging quick insights to find insights in your data. Q&A intelligently filters, sorts, aggregates, groups and displays data based on the question.

Visualize your data

There are thousands of techniques to visualize data in Power BI. Growing number of visualization types like Donuts, basic area, waterfall, filled maps, tree maps, funnel, gauges combo charts and more Custom visuals are also available from Power BI visuals marketplace There is also a facility to develop tools to develop, test, package new custom visuals Visualizations on report page are then connected – select value in one visualization to change other visualizations Full screen pop out mode for report visuals to show additional details.

Bring your story to life with DATA

You can save Power BI Desktop report files and easily publish them to powerbi.com Accessing dashboards using native mobile apps for Windows, iOS and Android Share if you feel appropriate with other Power BI users in your organization and packaging your reports in apps for easy consumption and control. Easily embed interactive Power BI visualizations in blog posts, websites, through emails or social media With Power BI Publish to web.
